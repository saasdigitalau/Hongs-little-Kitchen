Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Heart

Discover the soul of Asian cuisine, crafted with care in every dish.

Cooking with Love, Serving with Generosity

At Hong's Little Kitchen, every dish tells a story. From our hand-folded dumplings to our aromatic wonton noodle soups, we bring the authentic flavours of Asian cuisine to Point Cook with a philosophy rooted in quality, freshness, and genuine hospitality.

Our chef listens — crafting each plate with care, using time-honoured techniques and the finest ingredients. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a takeaway feast, you're not just a guest; you're family.

Free hot tea with every dine-in meal. Bike parking available. Come as you are, leave satisfied.

— Chef Hong
